Frequently Asked Questions
How do I place an order?
Visit our "Place Order" page, fill out the required information, and submit. You'll receive a tracking number to monitor your shipment.
How can I track my package?
Go to our "Track Package" page and enter your tracking number for real-time updates.
What are your delivery times?
Local deliveries: 1-2 business days. Long-distance: 3-7 business days. Express options available.
How is pricing calculated?
Pricing is based on distance, weight, dimensions, delivery speed, and extra services. You'll see the total before confirming.
What if my package is damaged or lost?
All shipments are insured. Contact support immediately for assistance with any issues.
Can I schedule a pickup time?
Yes, specify your preferred pickup time when placing an order. We'll do our best to accommodate you.
Do you deliver internationally?
Currently, we focus on domestic deliveries. Contact us for specific international requests.
How can I become a delivery partner?
Apply through our partner registration. We'll review your application and contact you with next steps.

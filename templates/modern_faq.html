Frequently Asked Questions
How do I place an order?
Visit our "Place Order" page, fill out the required information including pickup and delivery addresses, package details, and contact information. Once submitted, you'll receive a tracking number to monitor your shipment.
How can I track my package?
Go to our "Track Package" page and enter your tracking number for real-time updates on your shipment's location and status.
What are your delivery times?
Local deliveries typically take 1-2 business days, while long-distance shipments may take 3-7 business days. Express options are available for faster delivery.
How is pricing calculated?
Pricing is calculated based on several factors including distance, package weight and dimensions, delivery speed, and any additional services requested. You'll see the total cost before confirming your order.
What if my package is damaged or lost?
We take package safety seriously. All shipments are insured, and in the rare event of damage or loss, we'll work with you to resolve the issue promptly. Contact our support team immediately if you encounter any problems.
Can I schedule a pickup time?
Yes, you can specify your preferred pickup time when placing an order. We'll do our best to accommodate your schedule, and our delivery partners will contact you to confirm the exact pickup time.
Do you deliver internationally?
Currently, we focus on domestic deliveries within our service areas. We're working on expanding our international shipping capabilities. Contact us for specific availability in your region.
How can I become a delivery partner?
We're always looking for reliable delivery partners! You can apply through our partner registration process. We'll review your application and contact you with next steps. Requirements include a valid driver's license, vehicle, and clean background check.
